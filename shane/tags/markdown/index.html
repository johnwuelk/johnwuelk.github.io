Building in T.C.I.

Building in the Turks and Caicos Islands offers several attractive advantages:

Tax Haven: The Turks and Caicos Islands boast a tax-friendly environment. There’s no income, property, capital gains, or inheritance tax, making it an ideal location for those looking to maximize their investment returns.

Beautiful Location: With stunning beaches, pleasant weather year-round, and a thriving tourism industry, the islands offer a lifestyle advantage. Building here allows you to be part of a paradise destination.

Strong Infrastructure: The Turks and Caicos have a well-developed infrastructure with reliable communication networks, high-speed internet, and easy access to the US dollar as the main currency. This makes building and living there convenient.

Limestone

Limestone and sandstone are both popular building materials, but with key differences:

Durability: Limestone is denser and harder, making it more resistant to scratches and wear. Sandstone is softer and can be easier to work with, but may require more care to avoid damage.

Maintenance: Limestone generally requires less upkeep due to its lower porosity. Sandstone can be more prone to staining and needs careful cleaning to avoid using harsh chemicals.

Appearance: Limestone offers a classic, smoother look with fewer color variations. Sandstone boasts a wider range of natural colors and textures, with visible layering for a rustic feel.
